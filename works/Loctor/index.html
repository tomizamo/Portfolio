Loctor
PRODUCT DESIGN & SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT / CASE STUDY

Reading has gone digital, but organizing our files is stuck in the past. LOCTOR centralizes the chaos, giving you effortless sovereignty over your library on any screen.

Let’s be honest
For years, managing an independent ePub library was a niche for tech enthusiasts. The average user is forced to choose between restrictive corporate ecosystems or clunky, friction-heavy software. The reader deserves true sovereignty: the power to curate and govern their library, just as they once did with physical bookshelves.
Not Anymore.
LOCTOR was born to centralize this chaos. It is an open-source architectural response designed to transform dispersion into clarity. A fluid, distraction-free access point to preserve, organize, and experience written culture on any device. Welcome to your new library.
